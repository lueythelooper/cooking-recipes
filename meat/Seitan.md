# Seitan for Wings

Basic vegan seitan for fake chicken wings.

*not sure how much this makes*

---

- *2 cups* vital wheat gluten
- *1/2 cup* nutritional yeast
- Seasonings
   - *1 tsp* garlic powder
   - *1 tsp* onion powder
   - *1/2 tsp* cumin
   - *1/3 tsp* cayenne repper
   - *1/2 tsp* crushed rosemary
   - *1/2 tsp* crushed thyme
   
- Broth: *Pot* of veggie broth or water with *spoon* of veggie boullion

---

1. Bring pot of broth to a boil
2. Mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl
3. Add 1 cup of the veggie broth to dry ingredients
4. Mix until smooth ball of seitan dough
5. Let dough cool
6. Break into small pieces that are half of the desired end size
7. Let dry
8. Store, or pan/air fry until desired texture
