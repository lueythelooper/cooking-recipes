# Buffalo Wing Sauce

Chicken Wing sauce, basic recipe from the internet, passed down between generations spanning back to the time of the Roman Empire.

*1 bowl, covers a night's worth of wings*

---

- *2/3 cup* Frank's Buffalo Wing Sauce
- *1 stick* of salted or unsalted butter
- *1/2 tsp* honey
- Seasonings
   - *1 tsp* garlic powder
   - *1/4 tsp* cumin
   - *1/4 tsp* smoked paprika
   - *a few cranks* black pepper

---

1. Heat up franks wing sauce in a microwave bowl
2. Melt butter in separate bowl
3. Pour into same bowl
4. Add everything else
